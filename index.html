About Us

Progen DieselTech is a specialist and stocking importer and supplier of genuine, OEM, and quality spare parts for medium speed 4-stroke diesel engines. We have shops and warehouses in Bago City, Negros Island and in Calapan City, Mindoro Island. Our highly trained and experienced parts quality assurance engineers and mechanics assures correct and quality parts for customers.
